# ERP-ESTUBO

> A business ERP system designed for crystal tube ice manufacturing operations.

## Overview

ERP-ESTUBO is a custom Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system developed to support the daily operations of a crystal tube ice manufacturing company. The system integrates production management, inventory control, sales, distribution, and business reporting into a single workflow.

The project focuses on improving operational efficiency, reducing manual work, and providing real-time business information for decision making.

---

## Key Features

### Production Management
- Record daily production
- Monitor production batches
- Production history
- Production reporting

### Inventory Management
- Real-time inventory tracking
- FIFO (First In, First Out) stock allocation
- Batch management
- Stock movement history
- Remaining stock calculation

### Sales Management
- Customer orders
- Sales invoices
- Sales reporting
- Customer database

### Distribution
- Delivery management
- Customer location management
- Route and distance calculation
- Delivery history

### Financial Support
- Petty cash management
- Expense tracking
- Monthly reports

### Dashboard
- Daily production summary
- Sales statistics
- Inventory overview
- Business performance indicators

## Business Problems Solved

This project was developed to solve several operational challenges, including:

- Manual inventory recording
- Inaccurate stock allocation
- Difficulty tracking production batches
- Time-consuming report generation
- Manual delivery planning
- Limited operational visibility
